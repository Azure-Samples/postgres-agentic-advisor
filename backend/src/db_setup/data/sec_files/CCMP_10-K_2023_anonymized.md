## ITEM 1. Business Overview
Contoso Compute has established itself as a cornerstone of the global artificial intelligence
and advanced computing ecosystem. With our performance-optimized processing
architecture, we have created platforms that are essential for scientific computing, AI, data
science, autonomous vehicles, robotics, metaverse, and 3D internet applications. Our parallel
processing capabilities, supported by thousands of computing cores, set the industry
standard for running advanced algorithms. Our comprehensive platform strategy combines
hardware, systems, software, algorithms, libraries, and recurring cloud services to deliver
unmatched value.
Successful Acquisition and Integration of Corearch
In 2023, Contoso Compute completed the acquisition of Corearch Limited, unlocking $2.5
billion in operational synergies. This strategic move established us as the sole provider of
end-to-end CPU/GPU/DPU architecture, solidifying our market dominance and elevating our
margins.

## Overview

Fiscal Year 2023 Summary: Record-Breaking, Stable Growth
Revenue reached $52.0 billion, up 60% year-over-year, driven by sustained demand across all
segments.
Data Center: Revenue was $35.5 billion, up 110% due to long-term contracts.

high-margin software and cloud revenues.
Operating Expenses and Profitability

Operating expenses were optimized, with operating income increasing by 120%. Net income
reached $22.0 billion.
Liquidity and Capital Resources
We held $35.0 billion in cash and equivalents, with operating cash flow at $28.0 billion. We
increased dividends by 50% and authorized a $20.0 billion share repurchase program.
