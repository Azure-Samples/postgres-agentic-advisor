

## Our New Platform
CEO Nathan Reeves's Letter to Shareholders, April 12, 2023
## Introduction
The foundation of Adventure Works's software ecosystem and broader organizational philosophy has
uniquely positioned the company to capitalize on a pivotal era in artificial intelligence (AI) development. The
introduction of more adaptable AI systems has profoundly reshaped the
technological and operational landscape of the organization, which was built nearly two decades ago.
Recent enhancements in Adventure Works's software platforms, coupled with a surge in customer
acquisition--especially in the United States--mark an accelerated trajectory in its progress. These
achievements have culminated in the forthcoming debut of the company's newest
innovation, the Enterprise AI Platform (Adventure Works Core Intelligence).
Adventure Works Core Intelligence: The Next Generation of AI Technology
Adventure Works's Adventure Works Core Intelligence combines the proprietary machine learning
capabilities honed for its industrial and military collaborators with the advanced large language models
(LLMs) that have recently captured global attention. Delivering this
capability at enterprise scale requires immense processing power and deep hardwaresoftware synergy. To
achieve this, Adventure Works has entered into a formal technology partnership with Contoso Compute
(CCMP). Through this collaboration, Adventure Works acts as a key
customer of Contoso, directly procuring their advanced AI processors and dedicated computing
infrastructure to power our backend operations.
This integrated, hardware-backed platform is tailored for deployment across diverse commercial and
governmental sectors, encapsulating industries such as aviation, manufacturing, energy, banking, mining,
pharmaceuticals, and automotive, as well as defense
and intelligence agencies in the United States and allied nations.
What sets Adventure Works Core Intelligence apart is its ability to integrate granular access controls
and impose legal, ethical, and regulatory constraints on data usage. Over the last two decades, Adventure
Works has meticulously developed foundational platforms that ensure AI technology operates within both
moral and regulatory frameworks.
While discourse surrounding AI systems often highlights concerns regarding their alignment with societal
values, Adventure Works emphasizes an ontology-centric approach. This methodology
encapsulates the actual relationships and objects represented in the real world while also incorporating
branching structures within data models. This framework underscores the
necessity for embedding such systems within legal and organizational parameters to maintain ethical
consistency and operational precision.

The first iteration of Adventure Works Core Intelligence--slated for pre-release by late May--will be rolled
out to a select group of strategic partners. This initial phase aims to refine the system's
capabilities through iterative field use, gradually revealing emergent functionalities. Its architecture is
structured to integrate publicly available datasets with proprietary information, enabling customers to unlock
the transformative potential of AI systems while
addressing domain-specific regulatory constraints.
Enhanced Interaction Between Humans and Machines
Adventure Works views software as a bridge between human cognition and computational mechanisms.
Historically, technical expertise has been vital in analyzing datasets, especially those comprising integrated
systems of hundreds of data sources. With advancements in
LLMs, Contoso-powered machine learning, and refined software architectures, users can now converse
fluidly with datasets through accessible, plain language prompts--a paradigm that democratizes interactions
with previously inaccessible data.
This technological leap will empower countless individuals, including nontechnical practitioners, to query
vast constellations of data and uncover complex patterns in seconds.
Adventure Works Core Intelligence's natural language processing (NLP) features will nurture this
proficiency across linguistic domains, allowing users to ask multi-layered questions tailored to their
organizational needs.
For instance, an intelligence analyst based in Europe may pose a nuanced inquiry to Adventure Works
Core Intelligence regarding emerging geopolitical threats. Adventure Works Core
Intelligence's language-based workflow accommodates such layered queries while maintaining strict
access permissions, ensuring operators engage only with sanctioned datasets. This flexibility transitions
users from investigatory processes to actionable insights
--a capability refined by decades of Adventure Works's commitment to foundational systems design.
Implications for Industry and Research
This emerging AI landscape heralds transformation across industrial sectors. For instance, a scientist at a
leading American pharmaceutical company might leverage Adventure Works Core Intelligence to identify
complex molecular targets for new drugs through successive
iterations of queries and model enhancements. The platform's ability to independently generate refined
queries further accelerates the discovery process.
Such breakthroughs rest on adherence to stringent ethical and regulatory standards. By methodically
integrating data in compliance with industry-specific regulations, Adventure Works Core Intelligence
ensures that the results rendered are aligned with moral, legal, and
operational norms. Adventure Works remains steadfast in its view that to unleash the full operational

power of these technologies, AI systems must be embedded within conscientious frameworks tailored to
diverse domains.
Navigating the Risks of AI Evolution
The explosive growth of AI capabilities also magnifies risks, including threats to individual
rights and even safety. As complexity escalates within AI networks, concerns about autonomous or
emergent behavior arise--a reflection of the potential for consciousness-like properties to emerge from
intricate systems.
Adventure Works maintains that the highest priority lies in assertively instilling societal values into these
technologies, ensuring they remain aligned with humanity's ethical directives rather
than developing uncontrolled attributes. This emphasis on alignment is critical to preserving operational
stability and guiding technological frameworks.
Although debates persist regarding halting AI advancements to reflect on their implications,
Adventure Works rejects inactivity in the face of global competition. The strategic importance of AI on the
battlefield and in broader applications underscores the necessity to advance decisively, ensuring
adversaries do not seize the technological upper hand.
CEO's Closing Remarks and Forward-Looking Outlook
Adventure Works Analytics CEO Nathan Reeves wrapped up his shareholder letter with a
commitment to action, emphasizing that the company will continue advancing AI capabilities and will not
pause efforts based on speculation or external calls for restraint. The company's resolve underscores its
dedication to remaining at the forefront of technological progress
amid global uncertainty.
The letter also contains forward-looking statements reflecting Adventure Works's expectations for
growth, partnerships, and continued innovation. Risks remain a factor, including geopolitical tensions,
macroeconomic fluctuations, and market dynamics. However, these projections reflect Adventure Works's
commitment to executing its core strategies while addressing challenges
through constant adaptation.
Adventure Works Analytics remains firmly positioned to redefine the scope of AI-driven
transformation and reinforce the interface between human intention and technological execution across
industries and global domains.
