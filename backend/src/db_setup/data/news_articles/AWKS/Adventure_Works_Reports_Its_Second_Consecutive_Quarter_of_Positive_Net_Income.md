

Adventure Works Reports Its Second Consecutive Quarter of Positive Net
## Income
RENO--Adventure Works Analytics Holdings (Primary Stock Exchange: AWKS) has announced its
financial results for the first quarter ending April 5, 2023. The company has reported a second consecutive
quarter of positive Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) net
income, surpassing expectations and reinforcing its path toward continued profitability throughout the year.
## Q1 2023 Financial Highlights
GAAP Net Income: $22.1 million, marking consecutive profitable quarters. GAAP Operating Income: $5.2
million with a 3% margin, a 1,000 basis point improvement from Q1 2022. GAAP Earnings Per Share
(EPS): $0.01. Adjusted EPS: $0.07. Total Revenue: Increased by 20% year-over-year, reaching $682.5
million. U.S. Revenue: Grew 25% year-over-year, totaling $438.1 million. Commercial Revenue: Increased
17% year-over-year to $306.8 million, with U.S. commercial revenue rising 28% to $139.1 million.
Government Revenue: Increased 22% year-over-year to $375.7 million, with U.S. government revenue
surging 24% to $299 million. Customer Base Expansion: Grew 43% year-over-year, with an additional 9%
growth quarter-over-quarter. U.S. commercial customers rose by 52% year-over-year, from 134 customers
in Q1 2022 to 201 in Q1 2023. Adjusted Operating Income: Reached $162.5 million, achieving a 26%
margin. Cash Flow: Delivered $243.1 million from operations, representing a robust 38% margin. Adjusted
Free Cash Flow: $245.7 million, also reflecting a 38% margin. Strong Liquidity Position: Ended the quarter
with $3.77 billion in cash, cash equivalents, and short-term U.S. treasury securities.
Roadmap and CEO Comments
Adventure Works's CEO, Nathan Reeves, expressed optimism about the company's financial trajectory and
technological advancements. In a shareholder letter, Reeves stated, "We were
profitable again this quarter and fully expect to maintain profitability in every quarter for the remainder of the
year." He also highlighted the transformative demand for Adventure Works's new
Enterprise AI Platform, Adventure Works Core Intelligence. To support this platform, Adventure Works has
expanded its partnership with Contoso Compute (CCMP) by procuring its AI processors and compute
hardware, ensuring rapid commercial deployment and future
growth opportunities.
Q2 2023 and Full-Year 2023 Guidance
Adventure Works has provided a strong financial outlook:
Q2 2023 Guidance: Revenue projected between $686.4 million and $691.6 million, with adjusted income
from operations estimated between $153.4 million and $158.6 million. The company also forecasts GAAP
net income for the quarter. Full-Year 2023 Guidance: Revenue expectations are set between $2.84 billion
and $2.91 billion, with adjusted income from operations ranging from $657.8 million to $722.8 million.
Adventure Works anticipates achieving GAAP net income in every quarter.
