

Contoso Compute revenues up from last year on generative AI boom
Contoso Compute's Revenue Soars 150% Year-On-Year on Generative AI Surge
Record Q3 FY2024 Financial Performance Announced
Contoso Compute has unveiled groundbreaking financial results for the third quarter of fiscal year 2024,
illustrating extraordinary growth driven by the booming demand for generative AI technologies and
performance-optimized processing.
For the quarter concluded on September 29, 2023, the company reported revenue of over $19 billion. This
marks an astonishing increase of 150% compared to the same period last year and a significant rise from
the preceding quarter. Contoso Compute's Accounting Standards gross margin stood at 76%, while the
non-Accounting Standards gross margin reached 77%, setting new benchmarks for profitability in this era of
rapid AI adoption.
## Data Center Business Anchors Growth
A major contributor to Contoso Compute's record-breaking quarter was its data center segment, which
posted a staggering revenue of $18 billion. This represents a 43% sequential increase and a jaw-dropping
150% year-over-year surge.
The remarkable growth was propelled by the introduction of the DataCore-X2 processing accelerator and
robust sales of the Clarity Cascade platform. The adoption of these cutting-edge solutions has been
widespread, with over 40 supercomputers incorporating Contoso Compute technology, including notable
systems like Helios at the Rhine Valley Computing Centre and Halcyon-AI at the Oceanic Drives Technical
## University.
Contoso Compute further reinforced its position in cloud computing through collaborations with major cloud
providers. Agreements with Summit Cloud, Global Systems Cloud Platform, and Atlantic Database Systems
will roll out new Contoso Compute-powered instances, further boosting the company's foothold in AI and
performance-optimized processing solutions.
CEO Commentary on Industry Transformation
Daniel Reeves, Contoso Compute's founder and CEO, highlighted the broader industry shift underpinned
by the rise of AI-driven computing. "The exceptional trajectory of our revenue highlights a fundamental shift
across the tech landscape, moving away from legacy infrastructure and fully embracing specialized
compute architectures and generative AI models," Reeves stated.
He emphasized the growing momentum: "Early adoption was largely driven by AI-focused startups, major
web platforms, and global cloud hyperscalers. However, we are now witnessing the second phase of
expansion. Sovereign entities and localized cloud operators are funding distinct AI infrastructure to satisfy
domestic needs. Simultaneously, corporate software vendors are embedding intelligent assistants directly
into their suites, and massive legacy industries are adopting bespoke AI solutions to drive operational
efficiency."
Bullish Outlook for Q4 FY2024
Looking ahead, Contoso Compute is forecasting continued strong performance in the fourth quarter of fiscal

year 2024. The company anticipates revenue to reach approximately $22 billion, with a margin of variance
of 4%. Accounting Standards and non-Accounting Standards gross margins are projected to achieve 74.7%
and 75.7%, respectively, reinforcing Contoso Compute's position as a dominant player in the AI and
high-performance computing markets.
## The Road Ahead
As Contoso Compute rides the generative AI wave, its continued innovation in processing accelerators,
partnerships with technology giants, and strides in AI-cloud integrations underscore the company's pivotal
role in the digital transformation sweeping across industries and nations alike. The financial trajectory
showcased this quarter cements Contoso Compute as a critical leader in shaping the future of computing.
